- Browser is installed and WebDriver is configured.
- The Demo Blaze website (https://www.demoblaze.com) is accessible.
- The user has a valid account (already registered).

1. Open the browser and navigate to https://www.demoblaze.com
2. Click on the "Log in" button in the top navigation bar.
3. Wait until the login modal appears.
4. Enter the registered username in the "Username" input field.
5. Enter the correct password in the "Password" input field.
6. Click the "Log in" button.
7. Wait until the home page updates.
8. Verify that the text "Welcome" is displayed on the page.
9. Close the browser.
