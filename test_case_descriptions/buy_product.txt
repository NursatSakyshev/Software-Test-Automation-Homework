1. Open the browser and navigate to https://www.demoblaze.com
2. Click on the "Log in" button.
3. Enter a valid username and password.
4. Click the "Log in" button to sign in.
5. Wait until "Welcome" appears on the page.
6. Click on any product from the product list, for example, "Samsung galaxy s6".
7. Wait for the product detail page to load.
8. Click on the "Add to cart" button.
9. Handle the alert by clicking "OK" to confirm the addition.
10. Click on the "Cart" button in the navigation bar.
11. Wait for the cart page to load.
12. Verify that the selected product appears in the cart.
13. Click on the "Place Order" button.
14. Fill in the purchase form with the following data:
    - Name: name
    - Country: country
    - City: city
    - Credit card: credit_card
    - Month: month
    - Year: year
15. Click the "Purchase" button.
16. Wait until the confirmation message "Thank you for your purchase!" appears.
17. Click the "OK" button to close the confirmation.
18. Close the browser.
