- Browser is installed and WebDriver is configured.
- The Demo Blaze website (https://www.demoblaze.com) is accessible.

1. Open the browser and navigate to https://www.demoblaze.com
2. Click on the "Sign up" button on the top navigation bar.
3. Wait until the sign-up modal appears.
4. Enter a new username in the "Username" input field.
5. Enter a valid password in the "Password" input field.
6. Click the "Sign up" button inside the modal.
7. Handle the alert confirming that the sign-up was successful.
8. Close the browser.
